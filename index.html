Vision

Vox is a portable offline AI assistant powered by the Raspberry Pi it is designed to give you the power of modern AI without relying on the internet or cloud services unlike mainstream assistants that send your data to remote servers Vox does not steal your information there is no hidden logging or corporate tracking as a solo developer my goal is simple create an AI that is practical private and personal Vox is open in its development and belongs fully to the user.

What Vox Offers

Vox can communicate in many ways you can type to it and it will reply you can speak to it and it will understand you it can even speak back to you in many ways so you are never left hanging even without internet it handles text and voice naturally and everything stays on the Raspberry Pi keeping your data private and under your control it remembers recent conversations to respond more smoothly and the interface is fast simple and designed to run effortlessly on the Pi.

Why Vox Matters

Most AI assistants today are tied to corporate clouds they are fast but they take ownership of your data in the process Vox offers a different path AI that exists without strings attached for students creators developers and tech enthusiasts Vox represents the freedom of carrying a private assistant anywhere for people in areas with unreliable internet it proves that AI does not have to vanish when the connection drops for anyone.

Community

For updates progress reports and direct interaction the Discord community acts as Vox’s hub it is where milestones are shared challenges are discussed and supporters can help shape what comes next donors are recognized personally and every new feature or fix is shared openly Vox isn’t backed by a corporation it is a solo-developer project supported by people who believe in it that makes the community an essential part of its growth by donations.

Closing

Vox is not finished but it is real it already works as a proof of concept and each new stage pushes it closer to being a polished reliable assistant the vision remains clear an AI that is portable private transparent independent adaptable and optionally connected something you own something you control something that serves you on your terms.

Vox is AI without compromise...
